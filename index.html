About Me

I am a PhD student in the school of Computer Science and Engineering, a Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. I am also a researcher in the Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR), Singapore.

My PhD journey started in 2019, under the supervison of Prof. Kwoh Chee Keong and Dr. Wu Min.
I received my Masters degree in Computer Engineering from Tanta University, Egypt. I like working on Deep Learning with Biomedical Sensor data.
